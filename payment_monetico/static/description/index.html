Overview

Monetico is a french payment gateway module, developed by the french and canadian bank group Crédit-Mutuel (CM), CIC and Desjardins. Monetico is a more modern and up-to-date payment solution which replaces the CyberMUT and CM-CIC payment gateway (french payment solutions). Monetico includes all electronic transactions involved in card payment management (debit, credit, prepaid, etc.) and related transactions, along with the management of information exchanges between card issuers, cardholders, merchants and acquirers. It also covers the technologies that allow transactions made by phone or on in-store terminals, the Internet, mobile devices or other technological media. This solution is in the top 10 of the biggest e-payment company. More than 400,000 merchants uses the Monetico solution everyday, as Boulanger or Cdiscount (well-known e-commerce websites in France). The Monetico Payment module for Odoo allows usage of Monetico payment gateway on Odoo. Your customers will automatically be redirected to Monetico payment gateway while they will validate their order and will be able to pay using Vis or Mastercard.

  • This module is compatible with 3DSecure 2.0
  • This module is compatible with 3DSecure 1.0
  • Monetico is a fully functional payment gateway and provides many nationals or internationals credit cards:

    • CB / Visa
    • EuroCard / MasterCard
    • American Express (you should sign a contract with them)
    • PayPal (you should sign a specific contract)

Integration into the website

After completing the details of the shipping and billing, the next step is to validate the order. You can see Monetico as a method of payment. Select it and click Pay Now. After that, you will be redirected to the Monetico site.

Monetico payment pages

The Monetico payment pages are available in 9 languages, including english and french. The payment can be processed with these currencies:

  • Euros - EUR (€)
  • Dollars - USD ($)
  • Pounds Sterling - GBP (£)
  • Yens - JPY (¥)
  • Swiss francs - CHF

Operation

Once the order is validated, the customer is directed to the Odoo server where he fills out the payment form, a reply is sent to your Odoo. Depending on the code returned and your configuration, the command is validated.

Payment Transactions

In the Back-End, switch to Payment Transactions. All payments made using Monetico are recorded here. If you open a transaction, it will give you the details.
